Fire Emergency: Do's and Don'ts

Do's
Sound the Alarm: Alert others immediately.
Evacuate: Use marked exits to leave quickly.
Stay Low: Crawl if there's smoke to avoid inhaling it.
Close Doors: Shut doors behind you to slow the fire.
Use Stairs: Always take the stairs, not the elevator.
Call 911: Once outside, call emergency services.
Don'ts
Don't Use Elevators: They can be dangerous in a fire.
Don't Re-enter: Stay out until it's declared safe.
Don't Block Exits: Keep pathways clear.
Don't Open Hot Doors: Feel the door first; if hot, find another exit.
Don't Panic: Stay calm to think clearly.
Don't Ignore Alarms: Always evacuate immediately.
